Instructions for the Youngest in the Age of AI Dominance

Agata Hofman · TEDxGdańsk

In this TEDxGdańsk talk, FIRE Foundation co-founder Agata Hofman asks what kind of adults we are raising, and whether our education systems are truly preparing children for a world shaped by artificial intelligence. She argues that in an age of algorithms and automation, the most essential human qualities: empathy, curiosity, moral courage, and the capacity for genuine connection, more than ever. The video opens at a pivotal moment in her talk where she brings these ideas together.
